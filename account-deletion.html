AirOps Account Deletion

Request to delete your account and data

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your AirOps account and associated data, please send an email to our support team with the following information:

  • The email address associated with your AirOps account
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Optional: Reason for deletion (helps us improve)

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete the following data:

  • Your account profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Attendance records and check-in/check-out history
  • Face recognition data used for attendance verification
  • Device information and authentication tokens
  • Any saved preferences or app settings

Data Retention Period

Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving your request. Some data may be retained for a limited period as required by law or legitimate business purposes (e.g., legal compliance, fraud prevention).
